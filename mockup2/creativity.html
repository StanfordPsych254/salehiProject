Creativity Definition


Creativity experts agree that the essence of creativity is the ability to 'think outside the box.' That is, creativity involves coming up with an idea that is truly original and different from what has come before it. Creative people are those who have the ability to see the world differently from the average person, and to create things that don't conform to traditions.


Creativity Definition


Creativity experts agree that the essence of creativity is the ability to 'connect the dot.' That is, creativity involves coming up with an idea that synthesize or blends exsiting ideas in an original way. Creative people are those who have the ability to see the connection between seemingly disparate ideas, and to create things that bring those ideas together in a unique way.
